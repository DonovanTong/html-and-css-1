VANS Shoes Made In U.S.A.

The Van Doren Shoe that is widely known today was once produced in Anaheim, California, U.S.A. Materials used to make the shoes produced a far more durable and comfortable shoe. Vans solely produced in the U.S.A. until 1998 when production was moved to different countries in south asia. Although the shoes are still produced today, the comfort, look, and durability is inferior to those of past production in the U.S.A.
